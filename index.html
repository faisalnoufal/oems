Orphanage English Medium School

Orphanage English Medium School Palad is a prestigious venture of Edakkara Muslim Orphanage Committee which completed 23 years of glorious service in the field of education and social service. The committee was founded for the improved formation of society as well as the uplift of women and children. It began various educational institutions which became the instruments of social change and reformation. OEMS Palad is a minority institution of the local community established in the year 1997 with a welcoming, family atmosphere, trust, and friendliness. It is proven that the management has provided the students with satisfying facilities to be happy, to have high self-esteem, and to develop confidence in themselves and their abilities so that they can cope with new situations and accept the challenges that life offers.
