Co-curricular Activities

During the academic year 2019-2020, a good number of activities were carried out for the all-round development of our students.

“Njar Nadeel”

In order to get acquainted with the traditional method of cultivating rice our students were taken to the nearby paddy field. The method and preparation were well explained to our children by PTA President Mr. Varghese. Higher grade students and teachers joined him in the process of “njar nadeel”.

“Arts Day”

Arts festival, a festival encompassing a wide range of arts forms including dance, music, literature, poetry etc was held for two days with the active participation of over 90% of students competing in both individual and group items. The programmes had the participation of parents as well. The winners were awarded.

Children’s Day

It was yet another programme in the school. All the children starting from K G to class X were colourfully dressed up with flags in their hands. The procession started from the school premises to the KSEB junction with the active leadership of our PTA members, parents and teachers. The Children were greeted by EMORGHSS students and staff on the way. The “PAYASAM” at the end stirred more oomph and vigour to the children.

Sports Meet

Physical fitness paly an inevitable part in a child’s growth. Doing physical exercises and participation in the sports items enhance children to have better concentration and mindset towards their learning. The sports meet was held for two days and was inaugurated by the School Manager Mr. A Abdulla. Students gave their complete participation to all the items. Winners were awarded with bouquet, medals and certificates.

Yoga

Having a sound mind and sound body aids one to be physically fit and healthy. As per the instructions given by the CBSE one-week yoga programmes were held in our school. Students enjoyed and relaxed by participating in the events and expressed their readiness to continue the same. The week was concluded by a traditional competition “tug of war”.

Nabi Dhinam

The birth of Prophet Mohammed was celebrated very colourfully in our campus. The day began by reciting prayers, sweet distribution followed by Duffmuttu and many other cultural activities.

Keralappiravi

November first, KERALAPPIRAVI was celebrated in our school by the active participation in the road cleaning programme initiated by the Chief Minister of Kerala. Our students, by doing this activity proved that they are responsible in keeping the surroundings neat and clean.

Nattarivu

“To know about the past” was the main objective of this event as the modern generation have not witnessed many of the things that the old generation utilised in upbringing the present style. The culture, food items, things and medicinal herbs used in the past gave knowledge, curiosity and interest to our kids. All the antique items and medicinal herbs exhibited was collected by students, parents and teachers themselves. The people attended the exhibition shared a positive feedback.

Kids Festival K G to Class II

The kids festival for the academic year was held at Fathimagiri English Medium School, Nilambur. Around 32 students from L K G, UKG, Class I and Class II participated in the two days competition in both individual and group items with about nineteen CBSE schools in the Nilambur region. Our K G students secured Second Position in the Oppana and an overall hundred and ninety-two points in all..

Annual Day

The annual day “Razem 2K20” was organised with much homework, vigour and enthusiasm on 8th February 2020. The Chief guest of the day was Mr. Nishad Kiliyidukkil and Miss. Sruthi Saji being the celebrity guest. The day witnessed the inborn capacities of our young buds through their performance on the stage. The class proficiency award for the students were distributed on the occasion.

Football Tournament

Under 17 football for boys was arranged by Malappuram Central Sahodaya at I S S Senior Secondary School, Perinthalmanna. About 70 schools in the Sahodaya participated in it and our stars could qualify till the quarter final stage.

Friendly Football Match

An under 12 football match between N I C T Moothedam and O E M S Palad was played at our school football ground to expand the friendship between the schools both in academic and non-academic field. Both the teams played a stunning match and remained unbeaten. The trophies were awarded to the players by the managers of both the schools.

Charity

It is the sole responsibility of each human being to aid the needy. Hence, our children, teachers and PTA members jointly made their collection in serving two of the much-needed family members of OEMS.

Picnic

In addition to learning, our children were also given the opportunity to go out for enjoyment, fun and relaxation. Different classes chose different destinations for this purpose. K G to Class II students went one a one-day trip to “Kathir” at Pookkottumpadam and “Teak Museum” at Nilambur. Class III to VII students too went on a one-day trip to “Aathirappally” Thrissur. However, Students of Class VIII to X went on a three-day trip to Idukki, Wonderla and LULU Mall in Ernakulam.

Study Trip

Our students of class VII,VIII & IX were taken on a study trip to the Agricultural Research Institute at Aanakkampoyil, Manjeri as part of a project work they are initiated to do. The place instilled in them knowledge on preparing compost, tissue culturing, budding, grafting and many more.

Science Exhibition

We are living in an emerging country and science has a very dynamic role to play in the progress of any country or the world as a whole. Ergo, in order to make the children conscious of this fact, the science exhibition was held. Children came out with colourful ideas and items which increased our curiosity towards building new models and experiments.
